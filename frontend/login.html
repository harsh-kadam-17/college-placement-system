Terms and Conditions

XIE Placement Portal

Last updated: March 2025

By registering and using the XIE Placement Portal, you agree to the following terms:

1. Eligibility

Only registered students of Xavier Institute of Engineering (XIE) are eligible to use this portal as students. All information provided must be accurate and up-to-date.

2. Profile Accuracy

You are responsible for ensuring your CGPA, resume, and personal details are accurate. Providing false information may result in immediate disqualification and disciplinary action.

3. Resume Requirement

A valid Google Docs or Google Drive hosted resume link is mandatory before applying for any company. This ensures the Placement Cell can access and verify your profile at all times.

4. Application Policy

Each student may apply to each company's job role only once. Applications cannot be withdrawn once submitted. Multiple applications to the same role are not permitted.

5. CGPA Eligibility

Applications are subject to minimum CGPA requirements set by each company. The system will automatically enforce these. Your aggregate CGPA is computed from your four academic years.

6. Data Privacy

Your data is shared with recruiting companies participating in campus placement drives. By applying to a company, you consent to sharing your profile information with them.

7. Conduct

Students are expected to maintain professional conduct during all placement-related activities. Any violation may result in removal from the placement process.

8. Contact

For queries, contact the Placement Cell at placement@xie.edu.in.
